Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 12 cookies
IngredientsQuantity
Flour2 1/4 cups
Sugar3/4 cup
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix ingredients.
  3. Bake 9–11 minutes.
